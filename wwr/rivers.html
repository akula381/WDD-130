Rocky Waters - Rivers

The Salmon River, Idaho

The Salmon River, which is also known as "The River of No return" is a river which is located in center and easter part of idaho. It flows through the deepest wilderness of idaho. The salmon river is the longest river within a single state outside of Alaska. This River cuts through the heart of the Frank Church River of No Return wilderness.

The Blue River of Utah

The Blue river which is just outside of Wendover in tooele county. This river carries salt water to nearby evaporation ponds of potash which is designed for an industrial purpose.

Watson River of Arizona

Watson lake is a part of the Granite Dells. The dells are most unique areas where there are amazing granite formations are found. This beautiful lake which is located in the state of arizona is an oasis when you are looking to escape that horrible heat.
